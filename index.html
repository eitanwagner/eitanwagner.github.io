About
I'm an PhD candidate at HUJI in the field of Natural Language Processing. My research focuses on the analysis of narratives, with a special focus on Holocaust testimonies. I am part of "The future of the past" group. I'm also interested in probabilistic properties of languages, and in the intersection of language and cognition.
